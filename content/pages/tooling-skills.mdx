The repo includes a starter skill at `skills/unionize-organizing/SKILL.md`.

## What the skill is for

The skill tells agents when to use the guide corpus, when to search, when to use the pathfinder, and what safety boundaries they should not cross.

## What it reinforces

- no sabotage guidance
- no company-device organizing
- no pretending public guides are legal advice
- preference for careful fact comparison, trusted coworkers, and lawful next steps

## Why the skill matters

Even a good MCP surface can be used badly if the agent does not know the norms of the project. The skill bundle keeps the tooling aligned with the tone and boundaries of the site.
